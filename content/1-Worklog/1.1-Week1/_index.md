### Week 1 Objectives:

* Complete the onboarding process, familiarize with team members, company regulations, and workflows.
* Setup local development workspace (IDE, Git, credentials, SSH).
* Study fundamental Cloud Computing and AWS core services concepts.
* Setup a secure AWS Free Tier account with MFA and budget alerts.

* **Successful HR and Team Integration:** Completed all onboarding registration steps, set up default communication lines, and understood internal task workflow protocols.
