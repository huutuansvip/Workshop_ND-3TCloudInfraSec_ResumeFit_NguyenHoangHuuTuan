1. At the **Load Balancing** section, select **Target Groups**, click on the system's Target Group.
2. Switch to the **Targets** tab and observe the **Registered targets** table.
3. Confirm the `Health status` column of the instances displays a green `Healthy` state, proving the web server has started and is ready to receive requests from the Load Balancer.
