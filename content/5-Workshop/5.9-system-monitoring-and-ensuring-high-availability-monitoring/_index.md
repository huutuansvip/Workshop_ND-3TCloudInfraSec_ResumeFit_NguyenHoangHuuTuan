Once the system is running, administrators need a comprehensive and deep understanding of its actual performance. This section focuses on using Amazon CloudWatch to collect and track critical metrics such as EC2 CPU utilization, request counts, and ALB response times. Analyzing these metrics not only helps detect potential issues early but also serves as a basis for tuning auto-scaling Thresholds, ensuring the best user experience even during peak hours.
