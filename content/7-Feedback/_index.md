### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
I found the FCAJ environment to be highly academic yet very dynamic. The team frequently fosters engaging discussions on Cloud architecture, stimulating creative thinking. It would be even better if we could have cross-sharing sessions among different projects to broaden everyone's knowledge.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentors' guidance was crucial during my AWS 3-Tier architecture design phase. Instead of pointing out errors directly, they asked probing questions that encouraged me to dig into AWS documentation and make my own decisions. The admin team was equally professional, swiftly handling account and access issues.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The AI Resume Matching project perfectly aligned with the Cloud & DevOps path I am pursuing at university. Getting hands-on experience with EC2, ASG, and ALB made theoretical concepts much clearer and more practical than standard lectures.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
Beyond technical knowledge of system architecture and Auto Scaling, I sharpened my skills in risk analysis and cost optimization. Soft skills, such as presenting system designs to the team, have also improved significantly through regular progress reports.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The teamwork at FCAJ is fantastic. Whenever the system encountered errors or the AI pipeline changed, everyone jumped in to debug and brainstorm solutions without assigning blame. This solidarity served as a huge motivation for me to complete my tasks.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
Having access to AWS practice accounts and high-quality internal training sessions were incredibly valuable perks. The flexible schedule also allowed me to perfectly balance my project involvement with my university classes.

### Additional Questions
- What did you find most satisfying during your internship?  
  The aspect I value most is the opportunity to directly participate in designing and building the overall system architecture, rather than solely focusing on isolated programming tasks. This experience significantly enhanced my comprehensive understanding of software projects.
- What do you think the company should improve for future interns?  
  To better support incoming interns, the organization should consider developing and providing standardized onboarding guidelines. This would help new members quickly grasp and familiarize themselves with core AWS services during their initial phase.
- If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?  
  I would highly recommend this program to peers interested in Cloud Computing. The highly practical work environment demands proactive research and provides an ideal setting for developing professional skills.

  The organization could consider implementing more specialized training sessions focusing on specific AWS service domains (such as Serverless Computing or Cloud Security) to further broaden the interns' knowledge base.
- Would you like to continue this program in the future?  
  I am very much looking forward to the opportunity to continue collaborating and contributing to the subsequent development phases of the project.

  I would like to express my sincere gratitude to the management team and mentors at FCAJ for their dedicated guidance and for creating the most favorable conditions for me to successfully complete this internship.
